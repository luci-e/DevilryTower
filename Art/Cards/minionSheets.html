Assistant Coordinator
The Boss can be very busy sometimes… that’s why the Assistant Coordinators has been invented! Everybody knows they are running the hut.
HP: 6 XP: 6
Default attitude: Hostile.
Very good! Here, get the snack (passive): Any minion within 5 hexes, gain 1 additional hex of movement per tick.
Very well, Mr. Wu kærs: With a 1 tick action, a target minion within 5 hexes gains permanently 1 additional damage. Cooldown of 10 ticks.
Up to two Assistant coordinators per floor can have one of the following specializations.
Responsible: With a 1 tick action, any minion in a 5 hexes area changes his Attitude according to the responsible’s will. Cooldown of 10 ticks. The Responsible can commission the same task to any amount of minions within 5 hexes. There can be up to a Responsible for each floor.
Boss delegate: With a 1 tick action, a Boss delegate can change the job of any minion within 4 hexes (the equipment of the target remains unchanged). Cooldown of 10 ticks. A Boss delegate can commission tasks to any minion. There can be up to a Boss delegate for each floor.
