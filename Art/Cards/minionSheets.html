Accountant
HP: 3 + 1d4, MP: 1 + 1d2, EP: 2 + 1d3, XP: 4
Default stance: Loyal to No One, Neutral.
Minion - Administration and Finance

Attacks Smash them with a hammer!: As a 1 tick action, deal 1 damage to a single adjacent target. Put them in a box: As a 1 tick action, select an adjacent target to put them in a box. For the duration of 3 ticks, they take 2 additional damage from Smash them with a hammer!. Or… to save on postage: As a 1 tick action, poison an adjacent target for 1 poison damage, 15% chance they turn into a llama instead as if they drank Extract of Llama. Abilities Very good! Here, get the snack (passive): Any minion within 5 hexes, gain 1 additional hex of movement per tick. Very well, Mr. Wu kærs: With a 1 tick action, a target minion within 5 hexes gains permanently 1 additional damage. Cooldown of 10 ticks. Equipment Assistant Coordinators all start with Baby seal leather boots and Extract of llama. Up to two Assistant coordinators per floor can have one of the following specializations, with 2 additional XP in case of defeat. They cannot commission tasks to each other. Responsible: With a 1 tick action, any minion in a 5 hexes area changes his Attitude and Loyalty according to the responsible’s will. Cooldown of 10 ticks. The Responsible can also perform Kill them, they murdered the emperor: With a 1 tick action, they give the order to attack the same target to all minions loyal to the Villain they can assign tasks to. There can be up to a Responsible for each floor. Boss delegate: With a 1 tick action, a Boss delegate can change the job of any minion within 3 hexes, giving them the appropriate equipment and changing their stats immediately (damage is not healed this way). Cooldown of 10 ticks. There can be up to a Boss delegate for each floor.

Even villains need to keep their finances in order. The accountant’s job may seem tedious but it is a vital one. Villains don’t fear heroes nearly as much as they do the Evil Revenue Agency. Accountants make sure they never have to deal with the latter by guaranteeing all their taxes and fees are regularly paid.
